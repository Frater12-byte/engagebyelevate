Engage by Elevate: Online

Built for Today. Focused on What's Next.

Engage by Elevate, our pre-ATM event, has always been centered around connection, collaboration, and bringing partners together in a setting designed to strengthen relationships and drive business forward. Over the years, it has become a key platform where we connect with our top global tour operator network in a more personal and engaging environment, creating meaningful interactions beyond day-to-day business.

Introducing Engage Online, a forward-looking semi-online platform designed to bring together hotel partners, tourism boards, and tour operators to maintain momentum, strengthen relationships, and align for the future. It creates a focused, tech-enabled environment where partners can re-connect, continue conversations, and re-establish alignment across key markets and opportunities, ensuring engagement remains active and business continues to move forward.

Taking place over three days, from 2 to 4 June 2026, the programme will focus on the UAE across 2 and 3 June, followed by dedicated sessions across key destinations including Qatar, Thailand, and the Maldives on 4 June.

Our focus remains clear: looking ahead, supporting one another, and moving forward together. We've got your back, and we move with you into what comes next.

About Elevate World

A global travel & tourism group.

Elevate World is a UAE-based global travel and tourism group, redefining destination services across the UAE and the GCC countries, with operations extending into the Indian Ocean, Southeast Asia, and other key global destinations.

As a world-class organization, Elevate World's core strength lies within its top-tier team, and its significant state-of-the-art technology, adopting the most futuristic AI and digital solutions. Elevate World partners with some of the most renowned online and offline tour operators and wholesalers globally. Through a portfolio of specialized brands, the group provides seamless end-to-end solutions, including accommodation, tours and attractions, and comprehensive ground services.

The Elevate World portfolio includes Elevate Destination Management, GoElevate, ONELUX, Elevate Cruises, CONNECT Business Events, and Yalator, each playing a distinct role in delivering specialized travel solutions across the global tourism ecosystem.

Driven by a strong commitment to service excellence and continuous innovation, Elevate World has established itself as a market leader and one of the fastest-growing tourism groups globally, serving over 1 million travellers across 100+ destinations, through 10 regional hubs and a network of 300+ global partners.

Before You Register

What You Need to Know

  • Register using your company email address, which will serve as your login and cannot be changed. Personal email addresses will not be accepted.
  • Once registered, you will receive access to your dashboard, where you can browse attendees, request meetings, and manage your schedule.
  • Be sure to review your agenda regularly, as meeting slots will lock 48 hours prior to the event.
  • Registration is limited to one profile per hotel, agency or exhibitor. If additional profiles are required, please reach out to us directly and we will be happy to assist.
  • A meeting link will be automatically generated upon confirmation of each 1:1 appointment and can be added directly to your Outlook, Teams, or Google Calendar.
  • For participants attending in person in Dubai, the event will be hosted at the Elevate Tourism Hub in Al Khayat Avenue, Al Quoz, with dedicated private meeting areas arranged for all scheduled sessions.
  • Please ensure you have a laptop or desktop device, along with a headset or headphones, to take part in all sessions and scheduled meetings.
  • During the event, a dedicated tech team will be on-site to provide support.
