FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Engage by Elevate?
Engage by Elevate is a three-day matchmaking event connecting hotels and travel agencies through curated 20-minute virtual meetings. Held in Dubai, it brings together decision-makers from across the global travel industry.
Who can attend?
Hotels, travel agencies, and tour operators are invited to register. Tourism boards participate as featured presenters. Registration requires a company email address.
How do meetings work?
Once registered, you can browse the directory and request 20-minute meeting slots with any hotel or agency. The other side approves, and a Microsoft Teams link is automatically generated. Meetings are virtual — join from anywhere.
What happens after I register?
You will receive an email with a login link to your personal dashboard. From there you can complete your profile, upload your logo, browse participants, and start booking meetings.
Is there a cost to participate?
Registration for hotels and agencies is complimentary. Sponsorship packages are available for brands looking for premium visibility during the event.
Can I cancel or reschedule a meeting?
Yes, you can cancel or modify meetings up to 48 hours before the scheduled time. After that, slots are locked and changes are no longer possible.
