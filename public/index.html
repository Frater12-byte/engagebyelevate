About Elevate World

A global travel & tourism group.

Elevate World is a global travel and tourism group headquartered in Dubai, operating across the Arabian Peninsula, Indian Ocean, and Southeast Asia. With five specialized brands — Elevate DMC, Elevate Cruises, ONELUX, Elevate Hub, and Elevate Engage — the group delivers end-to-end solutions for leisure, luxury, MICE, and corporate travel.

Across 100+ destinations, 10 regional hubs, 300+ partners, and over 1 million tourists served, Elevate World connects the industry through technology, relationships, and curated experiences — including flagship events like Engage by Elevate.
