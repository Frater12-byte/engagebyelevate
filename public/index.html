About the Event

Three days of precision-matched meetings.

Engage by Elevate is a focused matchmaking event for decision-makers in travel and hospitality. Over three days, hotels and travel agencies are paired for 20-minute virtual meetings — each one scheduled through this platform and joined via Microsoft Teams.

Days 1 & 2 are dedicated to UAE-based hotels meeting agencies from around the world. Day 3 brings in international hotels — from Thailand, Qatar, and other key markets.

Between meetings, attendees join opening keynotes, tour operator sessions, and tourism board presentations. Every slot is designed to maximise the quality of connections you walk away with.

Before You Register

What you need to know.

  • Sign up using your company email address — this is your login and cannot be changed. Personal emails (Gmail, Yahoo, etc.) are not accepted.
  • Once registered, you'll receive an access link to your personal dashboard where you can view your agenda, book meetings, and manage your schedule in real time.
  • Check your agenda regularly. Meeting slots lock 48 hours before start time — after that, bookings and cancellations are no longer possible.
