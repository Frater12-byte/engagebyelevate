About the Event

Three days of precision-matched meetings.

Engage by Elevate is a focused matchmaking event designed for decision-makers in travel and hospitality. Over three days in Dubai, hotels and travel agencies are paired for 20-minute virtual meetings — each one scheduled through this platform and joined via Microsoft Teams.

Days 1 & 2 are dedicated to UAE-based hotels meeting with agencies from around the world. Day 3 brings in international hotels — from Thailand, Qatar, and other key markets — for a final round of high-value connections.

Between meetings, attendees join opening keynotes, tour operator sessions, and tourism board presentations. Every slot is designed to maximise your time and the quality of contacts you walk away with.

How It Works

Register. Browse. Meet.

Sign up in under two minutes, explore the directory of participating hotels and agencies, and request meetings at times that work for both sides. Approved meetings automatically receive a unique Teams link.

Before You Register

What you need to know.

  • Sign up using the email address you plan to use during the event. This is your login and cannot be changed once registered.
  • Once registered, you'll receive an access link to your personal dashboard where you can view your agenda, book meetings, and manage your schedule in real time.
  • Check your agenda regularly. Meeting slots lock 48 hours before start time — after that, bookings and cancellations are no longer possible.
